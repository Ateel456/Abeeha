Jobs in Web Development

Over 5,000 active job postings for web developers existed on Indeed.com at the time of this article, with an estimated average annual salary of over $91K in the United States. The average annual salary for front end web developers is even higher, at $102K. Practically every organization in existence is finding and interacting with people via the web, making the position of web developer one of the most sought after.

Web Development Process Stages

  1. Design: This is the fun part.
  2. Review: Once the team has had some time to work through the design process.
  3. Develop: Now that the design is approved, it is time for the website to go to the developer
  4. Test
  5. Launch!

Rules of web designing?

  • Focus on the message
  • Keep your layout neat
  • Choose user-friendly fonts
  • Avoid animation hell
  • Make it easy to navigate
